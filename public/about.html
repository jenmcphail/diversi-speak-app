A resource of diverse speakers for your next tech con

Equal Platform was inspired by Iheanyi Ekechukwuâ€™s list of diverse people in tech who want a platform.

Many times, people who plan conferences aren't developers and don't use or have access to github where the original list is located. My hope is that this app will be a resource to them when developing events, meetups, panels, and conferences to reach out to potential presenters and be inclusive of as many types of people as possible.
